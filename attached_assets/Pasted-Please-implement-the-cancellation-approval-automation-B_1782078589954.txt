2. Find the related booking using booking_id.

stripe.refunds.create({
payment_intent: booking.stripePaymentIntentId
})

5. Update the cancellation_requests row to APPROVED.
6. Update the booking status from CANCEL_REQUESTED to CANCELLED.
7. Update stripe_payment_status from paid to refunded after the Stripe refund succeeds.

When status is REJECTED:

1. Update the cancellation_requests row to REJECTED.
